Hackers Registration on Hackerslist Marketplace / Professional Ethical Hackers

Submit your resume or CV to global_support@hackerslist.co and one of our technical heads will get back to you for hackers registration. Please include a valid and up-to-date email address in your resume or CV.

You will be required to pass technical tests before vetting from the HR technical team.

Finally, you will need to deposit security money and sign several legal forms to be registered with the site terms.
